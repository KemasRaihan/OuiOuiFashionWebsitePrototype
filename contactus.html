FAQ

How do I pay for the product?

After completing the check-out you will be sent an e-mail with all the details regarding the payment.
You will have 1 hour to do the the payment either via Juice or any other bank transfer.
After the bank transfer is done you will need to get in touch with us either via email, live chat or through our social media and send us the screenshot confirming the payment.


When will my product be shipped or can I collect it from the shop?

Your product will be shipped within 1 working day from receiving the payment.
You can choose to collect your product from the shop during the opening hours - however, the payment needs to be done either via Juice or bank transfer prior to coming to the store.

How do I return an item?

You will need to get in touch with us as soon as you receive the product to describe the issue.
You will need to visit the shop to return the product within 5 working days from receiving it.
If you are a customer from Rodrigues you can ship the product back to us and then exchange it for a different product - you will need to cover the shipment fees for sending it back and for a shipment of a new product.


Can I reserve a product?

You can reserve your product. Simply get in touch with us.
